« The most delicious food motivated by a little secret ... a pinch of love is always thrown into it »


Our restaurant present a wide variety of dishes. If you are a vegetarian, don't be afraid to visit us, since we have a lot of yummy vegetarian dishes.

Our beef is 100% grazed and finished on natural grasses and is certified ‘Never Ever’. This guarantees they have ‘Never Ever’ been given Antibiotics, Hormones, Growth promoters or fed grain. The result is a healthy product with a natural taste, which is high in omega 3, Vitamin E.

Pasta fans will have a huge choice. Just a lot of pasta options: with cheese, tomatomas, korean and japanese noodles.

We know that soups are not popular at restaurant, but we still want to make our guests happy and offer delicious soups.

ABOUT US

LaLaFood from New York

LaLaFood is full of charm, charisma, elegance and known for its fine quality cuisine, high standards and an attentive team.

You will discover delicious food and cocktails in an elegant and intimate atmosphere.

LaLaFood is famous for it's open grill and magical summer nights.

The team

Director / James Crause

James Crause is one part director, one part chef, one part filmmaker. Funny and serious person at the same time.

Chef / Andrew Wong

Andrew Wong is attended cooking school in France at the Moulin De Mougins Restaurant on the French Riviera , worked in their kitchen and has been a personal chef to the stars including Miranda Kerr, Gwyneth Paltrow and Meg Ryan. He is trained in Vegetarian, Vegan and "Special Diets" and uses Organic foods when ever possible,.

Manager / Vivian Johnson

Vivian Johnson makes sure day-to-day operations run smoothly and efficiently. Their goal is to provide customers with a pleasant dining experience that lives up to brand standards while also protecting profitability.
